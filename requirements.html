Requirements

(Numerical) requirements to be used during testing.

1) 2-3 short sentences accompanied by pictures of instructions, pasted on the hermitic box and the braking system

2) Rope is 100 ft long (for a 10-story building)

3) All components must be able to withstand 50 ~ 500 lb weight (100 ~ 250 lb person, x2 safety factor)

4) The maximum velocity the system will operate is 3 ft/s

5) The system will weight less than 10 lbs

6) The harness/belt can hold up to a body circumference of 75 in

7) All components in the system can withstand a maximum temperature of 1000 degrees Fahrenheit
